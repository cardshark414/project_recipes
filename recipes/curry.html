Thai Chicken Curry

Return to Homepage

Description

Think restaurant-quality chicken curry is only available for takeout? Think again! You can pull together this incredibly tasty Thai Chicken Curry with Coconut Milk in less than 30 minutes and the flavor is so, SO good.


Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Red Curry Paste
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Unsweetened Coconut Milk
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Corn Starch

Steps

  1. Add chicken, peppers, and onions to skillet. Drizzle with oil
  2. Add curry powder and mix
  3. Add coconut milk, curry paste, pepepr flakes, and salt
  4. Mix well
  5. Cover to simmer
  6. Mix corn starch and water, then add to pan and mix well to incorporate
  7. Season to taste and remove from heat
